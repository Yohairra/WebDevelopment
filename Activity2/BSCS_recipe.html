Pinoy Handaan

Pika-Pika Pangunahing Handa Merienda

Lumpiang Shanghai

Spaghetti

Maja Blanca

Ingredients
  • 50 pcs lumpia wrapper
  • 3 cups cooking oil

    • Filling Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lbs ground pork
  • 2 pcs onion minced
  • 2 pcs carrots minced
  • 1/2 cup parsley chopped
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 2 8oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 3oz can potted meat
  • 1 knorr beef cube
  • 1 1/4 cups banana ketchup
  • 1 medium yellow onion diced
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 3 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 pcs sliced hotdog
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 4 coconut milk
  • 3/4 cup cornstarch
  • 14oz condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup fresh milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 15oz whole sweet kernel corn
  • 5 tbsp toasted grated coconut

Procedure
  1. Combine all filling ingredients in a bowl
  2. Scoop around 1 to 1 1/2 tbsp of filling and place over a piece of lumpia wrapper
  3. Spread the filling and then fold both sides of the wrapper
  4. Fold the bottom side of the wrapper
  5. Brush beaten egg mixture on the top end of the wrapper
  6. Roll-up until complete wrapped
  7. Perform steps 2 until 6 until all mixture are consumed
  8. Heat oil in a cooking pot. Deep fry the lumpia in medium heat until it floats
  9. Remove from the pot and let the excess oil drip
  1. Boil water in a cooking pot. Add salt and cooking oil
  2. Put the spaghetti into the pot. Cook according to package instructions
  3. Remove the spaghetti from the pot. Drain the water and set aside
  4. Heat oil in a pan. Saute garlic and onion
  5. Add ground pork. Cook until the color turns light brown
  6. Put-in the potted meat. Stir and cook for 2 minutes
  7. Pour tomato sauce and water. Stir and let boil
  8. Add the beef cube. Stir and cook for 5 minutes
  9. Put-in the banana ketchup. Stir and cook for 5 minutes
  10. Season with salt, ground black pepper, and sugar
  11. Put-in the hotdog. Stir and cook for 5 minutes
  12. Arrange the spaghetti in a serving plate
  13. Pour the sauce over the spaghetti
  14. Top with shredded cheddar cheese
  1. Pour the coconut milk in a cooking pot and bring to a boil
  2. Add the sugar, condensed milk, and whole sweet kernel corn then stir until all the ingredients are evenly distributed
  3. Simmer for 8 minutes
  4. Combine the milk and cornstarch then whisk until the cornstarch is diluted
  5. Pour the fresh milk and cornstarch mixture in the cooking pot and stir thoroughly
  6. Simmer until the texture becomes thick
  7. Transfer the mixture to a mold and let it cool down
  8. Refrigerate for about 1 to 2 hours
  9. Slice and serve on a serving plate
  10. Sprinkle with toasted grated coconut
